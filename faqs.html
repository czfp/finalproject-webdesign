Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are your shipping options?
We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days.
Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied?
Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for products in their original condition.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order using this number on our website.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within the Philippines. We are working to expand our international shipping options in the future.
