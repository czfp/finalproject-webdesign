About Us

NATURE REPUBLIC believes in the power of nature. Founded in 2009, the Korean beauty brand takes on a naturalistic approach by discovering pristine ingredients from around the world and presenting them in their most natural states. Though not certified as organic or natural, its products focus on the use of nature's best gifts such as Californian aloe vera, Moroccan argan, African shea butter, French snail secretion, Bulgarian rose, Polynesian lagoon water and Jeju sparking water.


Priding itself as an affordable and diverse brand, NATURE REPUBLIC offers an extensive collection of products ranging from skin care and makeup essentials to hair care, body care, beauty tools, nail varnishes and grooming products. NATURE REPUBLIC's aloe vera gel, in particular, is its most popular star item. The brand also successfully appeals to the younger generation and to fans of Korean music and dramas by appointing K-pop and Korean Wave stars like EXO, Rain, JYJ, Girls' Generation's Taeyeon, Shin Se-kyung and Jang Geun-suk as its ambassadors.
